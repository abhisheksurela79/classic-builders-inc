About Classic Builders Inc

We Specialize in Transforming Spaces and Simplifying Lives For Our Clients

Classic Builders Inc. is a full-service general contractor in Sun Valley, San Fernando, and San Gabriel Valley, CA. We specialize in building and remodeling services such as design-build, residential, concrete, plumbing, and more. We know all the ins and outs of home building. Our hands-on approach and commitment to top-quality workmanship make us stand out.

Whether a minor renovation or complete home construction, our general contracting services ensure timely completion and excellent results, leaving our clients satisfied with their newly renovated space. We offer free in-person estimates for people living in Sun Valley and the surrounding areas.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to build homes and remodel spaces that improve our clients' comfort. We ensure timely completion and excellent results, leaving our clients satisfied with their newly renovated spaces.

Our Vision

Our vision is to help our clients receive the highest quality of general contracting services. We help create the most effective solutions that help our clients bring their visions to life.
